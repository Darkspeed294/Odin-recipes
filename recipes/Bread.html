How to make artisan bread

Making artisan bread is a lazy, easy and fun way to make your own bread. This page will describe you how to make your own artisan bread within your own kitchen!


The required ingredients are as followed:
  • Flour 450 grams
  • Yeast 2 tsp
  • Kosher salt 2 tsp
  • Lukewarm water 375 ml

Instructions

  1. Mix the ingredients inside a bowl using a spatula or dough mixer
  2. Cover the bowl with cling wrap and let it rise for 2-3 hours near a warm place
  3. after the dough has risen for 2-3 hours, refrigirate the dough for 12 hours, up to 3 days
  4. preheat the oven with the pan you use to bake the bread at 230c
  5. sprinkle the parchement paper with dough and transfer the dough onto the parchement paper
  6. put the dough inside the preheated pot and bake for 30 minutes with the lid on
  7. after 30 minutes, remove the lid and bake the bread for 12 minutes or until golden brown
  8. after removing, let the bread cool down for 10-20 minutes
